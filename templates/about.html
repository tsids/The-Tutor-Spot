The Tutor Spot is a non-profit free tutoring service. Students can sign up, choose their tutor, and begin to learn live from people around the world. When you find a tutor you like, feel free to apply to that tutor so that you can message them. Tutors can also register and teach their students.

Our mission is to make education affordable and accessible for people from all over the world while using the most up-to-date technologies.
